Title: Relative URL support added 
Author: Horst Prote <prote@fmi.uni-stuttgart.de>
Posted: 2021-06-22
Revision: 1
News-Item-Format: 2.0

With version 13.12.5 support for installing GitLab under a relative URL was added.
It can be enabled it with the new USE flag 'relative_url'.

Many thanks to Amber Schenck who supplied the patch.
